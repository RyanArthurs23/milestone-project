ABOUT THE BAND

Green day is a Punk Rock band formed by Billie Joe Armstrong (lead vocalist and guitarist) and Mike Dirnt (bassist) in 1986 who were later joined by Tre Cool (Drummer). Since 1985 Green day have gone on to sell more than 85 million records worldwide including 5 UK top 10s singles and 3 number 1 UK albums. Green day have also gone on the pick up other awards such as the best international album award at the Brits in 2006, a Grammy for the best rock album 21st century breakdown and many more. It is with out a doubt that green day are a very popular rock band with songs still breaking in to the charts today.
